## Who We Are

Particle Post ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website [theparticlepost.com](https://theparticlepost.com). We publish twice-daily briefings on artificial intelligence in business and finance.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: **privacy@theparticlepost.com**

---

## What Data We Collect

### Analytics Data (Google Analytics 4)

Only if you **accept cookies**, we collect the following data via Google Analytics 4:

- Pages visited and time spent on each page
- Approximate geographic location (country/region level, derived from anonymised IP)
- Browser type, operating system, and device category
- Referral source (how you found us)

We do **not** collect your full IP address — IP anonymisation is enabled.

We do **not** collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information through analytics.

### No Account Data

Particle Post does not require account registration. We do not collect passwords, payment information, or user profiles.

---

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For visitors in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process analytics data on the basis of **explicit consent** (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR). You provide consent by clicking "Accept Analytics" in the cookie banner.

If you decline, no analytics cookies are set and no data is sent to Google.

## Cookies

We use the following cookies **only after your consent**:

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|----------|---------|----------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics | Distinguishes users | 2 years |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics | Distinguishes users | 24 hours |
| `_ga_XXXXXXXX` | Google Analytics | Session state | 2 years |

See our [Cookie Policy](/cookies/) for full details and instructions on how to delete cookies.

## How We Use Your Data

Analytics data is used solely to:

- Understand which articles are most useful to readers
- Identify technical issues (e.g. pages that fail to load)
- Inform editorial decisions (popular topics, peak reading times)

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your data with any third party except Google Analytics for the purposes described above.

---

## Data Retention

Google Analytics retains event data for **26 months** by default. You may request deletion by contacting us.

---

## Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the EEA, you have the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of data we hold about you
- **Erasure** — request deletion of your data
- **Object** — withdraw consent at any time (simply clear cookies or click "Decline" in the cookie banner on a new visit)
- **Portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Lodge a complaint** with your national data protection authority

To exercise any right, contact: **privacy@theparticlepost.com**
