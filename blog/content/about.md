## What Is Particle Post?

Particle Post is an AI-powered publication covering the intersection of artificial intelligence, business strategy, and financial markets. We publish twice daily — morning and evening — so you never miss a signal.

## Who Is It For?

Executives, analysts, investors, and operators who need to understand how AI is reshaping their industries — without wading through hype or academic jargon.

## How It Works

Every article is researched, written, edited, and SEO-optimized by a purpose-built AI agent crew running on Claude (Anthropic). Articles reference current news, cite real data, and are reviewed for accuracy before publication.

## Contact

For inquiries, reach out via the RSS feed or open an issue on our GitHub repository.
