## Heading Structure Guidelines

### H1 — Article Title (set in frontmatter `title:`)
- **Must contain the primary keyword or a close variation**
- Pattern: `[Primary Keyword]: [Specific Context With Named Company/Figure/Number]`
- Include year (2026) when relevant to SEO — time-sensitive queries convert well
- Max 70 characters for full display in Google SERPs
- ✅ Good: `"AI in Banking: JPMorgan's $1.5B Investment Reshapes Risk Management in 2026"`
- ✅ Good: `"Machine Learning Credit Scoring: How Goldman Sachs Outpaces Traditional Models"`
- ❌ Bad: `"The Future of AI in Finance"` — too vague, no entity, no specificity

### H2 — Section Headings (## in markdown)
- Use **LSI keywords** — synonyms and semantically related terms, not exact keyword repetition
- **Question format** for featured snippet potential: "How Does AI Detect Fraud in Real Time?"
- **"The [X] Approach"** format works well: "The JPMorgan Approach to AI Compliance"
- Each H2 should be answerable as a standalone section — Google may pull it for featured snippets
- Minimum 2 H2 headings per article (Production Director requirement)
- ✅ Good: `"## How Machine Learning Is Replacing Credit Analysts at Major Banks"`
- ✅ Good: `"## The Regulatory Challenge: AI Decisions and Explainability Requirements"`
- ❌ Bad: `"## More Details"` or `"## Background"` — not keyword-rich, no SEO value

### H3 — Sub-headings (### in markdown)
- Use for **long-tail keyword targeting** within a section
- More specific than H2 — name a company, method, or specific metric
- ✅ Good: `"### Goldman Sachs Marcus: AI-Driven Loan Approval in Under 60 Seconds"`
- ✅ Good: `"### Basel IV Compliance: What AI Systems Must Explain to Regulators"`

## Meta Description Pattern

Structure: `[Primary keyword near start] + [specific hook/data point] + [benefit to reader]`
- Must include primary keyword in first 60 characters
- End with a clear benefit or call to curiosity
- Max 155 characters — Google truncates beyond this
- ✅ Good: `"AI in finance is reshaping credit risk at JPMorgan. Here's what the $1.5B investment means for institutional investors."`
- ❌ Bad: `"Learn about artificial intelligence and how it affects banking and financial services in today's world."` — generic, no hook
