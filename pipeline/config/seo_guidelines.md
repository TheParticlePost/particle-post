## 4. H1 PATTERN

**Formula:** `[Primary Keyword]: [Named Entity (Company / Figure / Dollar Amount)] + [Specific Outcome or Tension]`

**Rules:**
- Maximum **70 characters** for full SERP display
- Lead with the keyword, not the company name
- Include the year (2026) when the article covers a trend or forecast
- Never start with a question

**Examples:**
- `AI Fraud Detection: JPMorgan Cuts False Positives 40% in 2026`
- `Agentic AI Finance: Goldman's Compliance Gap Costs $2B`
- `Machine Learning Credit Scoring: How Banks Are Repricing Risk`
- `AI Risk Management Finance: Fed Guidance Reshapes Bank Models`

---

## 5. H2 PATTERNS

### Template A — LSI Keyword Enrichment
`How [Named Company/Sector] Is Using [Specific AI Method] to [Measurable Outcome]`
> *Example: How Citigroup Is Using Machine Learning to Cut Credit Losses by 18%*

### Template B — Featured Snippet / Question Format
`What Does [AI Development] Mean for [Specific Audience] in [Year]?`
> *Example: What Does Agentic AI Mean for Bank Compliance Officers in 2026?*

### Template C — Named Entity Authority Format
`The [Company] Approach to [AI Use Case]: [Brief Verdict or Data Point]`
> *Example: The JPMorgan Approach to AI Risk Management: Three Lessons for Mid-Market Banks*

**Guidance:** Rotate all three templates within a single article. Template B is highest priority when targeting featured snippets on long-tail queries. Template A drives LSI keyword density without over-optimization.

---

## 6. H3 GUIDANCE

H3s should be granular, company- or metric-specific, and naturally embed long-tail keywords:

- `[Specific Bank/Firm]'s [Dollar Amount / Percentage] Investment in [AI Method]`
- `Why [Regulatory Body] Flagged [Specific AI Practice] in [Year]`
- `The [Number]-Step Process [Company] Uses for [AI Workflow]`
- `[AI Capability] vs. [Legacy Process]: The Efficiency Gap by the Numbers`

**Rule:** Every H3 must name at least one: a company, a regulator, a dollar figure, or a percentage. Generic H3s like "Benefits of AI" are not permitted.

---

## 7. META DESCRIPTION PATTERN

**Formula:** `[Primary keyword in first 60 chars] + [specific data point] + [reader benefit or forward-looking hook]`

**Max length:** 155 characters
**Required elements:** keyword anchor early, one hard number, one action/insight signal

**Examples:**
- `AI fraud detection in banks is evolving fast — JPMorgan's real-time ML system cut losses by $400M in Q1 2026. Here's what every risk officer needs to know.` (154 chars)
- `Agentic AI compliance fintech: regulators are struggling to keep pace. We break down the three gray zones every CFO must address before Q3 2026.` (144 chars)
- `Machine learning credit scoring is reshaping bank lending — Experian's 2026 data shows a 22% default-rate reduction. What this means for your credit book.` (154 chars)
