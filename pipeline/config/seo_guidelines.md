## 4. H1 PATTERN

**Formula:** `[Primary Keyword]: [Named Company/Figure/Stat + Specific Context]`

- Max 70 characters for SERP display
- Include year (2026) when the topic is time-sensitive or regulatory
- Lead with the keyword, not the company name

**Examples:**
- `AI Agent Governance: 5-Step Enterprise Control Framework 2026` (60 chars)
- `Agentic AI Finance: What Oracle Proves About Execution ROI` (58 chars)
- `AI Fraud Detection Banks: The 2026 Arms Race CFOs Must Win` (58 chars)
- `EU AI Act Compliance Banking: What Goes Live This Quarter` (57 chars)

---

## 5. H2 PATTERNS

**LSI/Topical Pattern:**
- `How [Technology/Method] Reduces [Specific Risk] in [Context]`
- `The [Named Company] Model for [Keyword Topic]`
- `Why [Audience] Cannot Ignore [Development] in [Year]`

**Featured Snippet (Question Format):**
- `What Does [Primary Keyword] Mean for [Audience] in 2026?`
- `How Does [Method] Work in [Specific Finance Context]?`
- `Which AI Governance Framework Works for Enterprise Banks?`

**Named Entity Format:**
- `The JPMorgan Approach to AI Risk Management`
- `How Oracle Fusion Handles Agentic AI Compliance`
- `What the EU AI Act Requires of Financial Institutions`

---

## 6. H3 GUIDANCE

H3s should be long-tail specific and name a company, method, metric, or regulation:

- Name the specific tool, vendor, or platform being discussed
- Include a metric or timeframe wherever possible
- Examples:
  - `Basel IV and Machine Learning Credit Scoring: What Changes in 2026`
  - `Goldman Sachs AI Fraud Detection: 3 Lessons for Mid-Market Banks`
  - `Agentic AP Automation: The 60-80% ROI Benchmark Explained`
  - `EU AI Act Article 6: High-Risk Classification for Financial Models`

---

## 7. META DESCRIPTION PATTERN

**Formula:** `[Primary keyword in first 60 chars] + [specific data point] + [reader benefit/action]`
- Max 155 characters total
- Never start with the site name
- Use active voice; avoid passive constructions

**Template:**
`[Keyword]: [Specific stat or named development]. Learn how [audience] can [specific benefit] — [differentiator].`

**Examples:**
- `AI agent governance framework: enterprise deployments without controls fail 3x more often. Five-step plan for CFOs and CIOs.` (125 chars)
- `Agentic AI finance: Oracle and Zalos show 60-80% AP automation ROI is achievable. Here is how to build your business case.` (122 chars)
- `EU AI Act compliance banking: enforcement begins Q2 2026. What financial institutions must do before the first penalty lands.` (124 chars)
