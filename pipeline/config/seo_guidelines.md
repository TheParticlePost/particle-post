## 4. H1 PATTERN

**Formula:** `[Primary Keyword]: [Named Company or Figure or Dollar Amount] + [Specific Context]`

**Rules:**
- Maximum 70 characters for full SERP display
- Include year (2026) only when the story is explicitly time-bound
- Must contain one primary keyword from Section 2
- Avoid clickbait framing — lead with the most specific fact

**Examples:**
- `AI in Finance: JPMorgan's $1.5B Automation Push Targets Back Office` *(68 chars)*
- `Artificial Intelligence Banking: Goldman Sachs Cuts 3 Risk Roles in 5` *(69 chars)*
- `Agentic AI Finance: SEC Flags Compliance Gap at Three Major Lenders` *(67 chars)*
- `Fintech AI: Stripe's New Fraud Engine Blocks $2B in Annual Losses` *(65 chars)*

---

## 5. H2 PATTERNS

Use 3–4 H2s per article. Rotate among these three templates:

**Template A — LSI Keyword Pattern (adds semantic depth):**
> `How [Named Company/Sector] Is Using [Specific AI Method] to [Measurable Outcome]`
- Example: `How JPMorgan Is Using Machine Learning Credit Scoring to Cut Default Rates`

**Template B — Question Format (featured snippet targeting):**
> `What Does [AI Topic] Mean for [Specific Finance Role or Institution Type]?`
- Example: `What Does Agentic AI Mean for Compliance Officers at Regional Banks?`
- Example: `How Does AI Fraud Detection Work in Real-Time Payments?`

**Template C — Named Entity / Institution Format:**
> `The [Company] Approach to [AI Finance Topic]`
- Example: `The BlackRock Approach to AI Risk Management in Portfolio Allocation`
- Example: `The Fed's Framework for AI Compliance Financial Services Oversight`

---

## 6. H3 GUIDANCE

H3s should be specific, granular, and company/metric/method-named. They are the primary vehicle for long-tail keyword placement.

**Patterns:**
- `[Company]'s [Metric]: What the Numbers Show`
  - Example: `Visa's Fraud Detection Rate: What the Q1 2026 Numbers Show`
- `[AI Method] in [Specific Finance Function]: A Step-by-Step View`
  - Example: `Machine Learning Credit Scoring in Auto Lending: A Step-by-Step View`
- `Why [Regulation or Regulator] Is Targeting [AI Finance Practice]`
  - Example: `Why the FCA Is Targeting Agentic AI Compliance Gaps in 2026`
- `[Dollar Amount or Percentage]: The Cost of [AI Finance Problem or Benefit]`
  - Example: `$4.6B: The Cost of AI-Related Compliance Failures in European Banks`

---

## 7. META DESCRIPTION PATTERN

**Formula:** `[Primary keyword in first 60 chars] + [specific data point or named company] + [reader benefit — what they'll learn or decide]`

**Rules:**
- Maximum 155 characters total
- Keyword must appear in the first 60 characters — this is what Google bolds in SERPs
- Include one number or named entity — this lifts CTR
- End with a reader-benefit clause (what they'll understand or be able to do)

**Template:**
> `[Keyword]: [Named entity] [did/announced/reported] [data point]. Here's what it means for [reader role].`

**Examples:**
- `AI in finance: JPMorgan's new ML credit model cut defaults 18% in Q1. Here's what it means for lending strategy.` *(114 chars)*
- `Agentic AI finance: SEC flagged three lenders for autonomous compliance gaps. What CFOs and risk teams need to know.` *(116 chars)*
- `Fintech AI infrastructure: 80% of firms have committed to AI-first ops, per new survey. What laggards risk by 2027.` *(117 chars)*
