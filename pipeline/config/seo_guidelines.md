## 4. H1 PATTERN

**Formula**: `[Primary Keyword]: [Named Company or Figure or Dollar Amount] + [Specific Context]`

**Rules**:
- Maximum 70 characters for full SERP display
- Include year (2026) when the article is time-sensitive or trend-based
- Must contain at least one primary target keyword from Section 2
- Avoid hype adjectives (revolutionary, unprecedented, game-changing)

**Examples**:
- `AI Risk Management Finance: How JPMorgan Is Rebuilding Its Credit Stack`
- `Agentic AI Finance: Goldman Sachs Pilots Autonomous Compliance in 2026`
- `Machine Learning Financial Services: BlackRock's $2B Model Overhaul`
- `AI Fraud Detection Banks: Visa's Real-Time System Blocks $4B Annually`
- `Fintech AI: Why 80% of Banks Are Rebuilding Core Infrastructure`

---

## 5. H2 PATTERNS

Use 3–4 H2s per article. Mix the three formats below for LSI coverage and featured snippet eligibility.

### Format A — LSI Keyword Pattern (topical depth)
`[Named Entity]'s Approach to [Specific AI Function]`
- Example: `Citigroup's Approach to AI-Driven Credit Scoring`
- Example: `The Fed's Framework for Machine Learning Model Risk`

### Format B — Question Format (featured snippet target)
`What Does [Trend/Technology] Mean for [Specific Audience]?`
`How Are [Named Institutions] Using [AI Method] in [Year]?`
- Example: `What Does Agentic AI Mean for Bank Compliance Officers?`
- Example: `How Are Hedge Funds Using AI Trading Algorithms in 2026?`

### Format C — Outcome/Data Format (authority signal)
`[Metric or Dollar Amount]: The Real Cost/Return of [AI Application]`
- Example: `$1.2 Billion Saved: The Real Return on AI Fraud Detection`
- Example: `34% Faster Settlements: How AI Is Reshaping Payments Infrastructure`

---

## 6. H3 GUIDANCE

H3s should be specific — name the company, method, metric, or regulation. Avoid generic section titles.

**Good H3 examples**:
- `JPMorgan's COiN Platform: 360,000 Hours of Legal Work, Automated`
- `Basel IV Compliance: Where Machine Learning Risk Models Fall Short`
- `Credit Scoring at Scale: How Upstart's Model Outperforms FICO`
- `The SEC's 2026 Guidance on Algorithmic Trading Disclosures`
- `Agentic AI in Practice: HSBC's Autonomous AML Workflow`

**Avoid**:
- `Benefits of AI` / `Key Takeaways` / `What This Means` — too generic, no SEO value

---

## 7. META DESCRIPTION PATTERN

**Formula**: `[Primary keyword in first 60 chars] + [specific data point or named entity] + [reader benefit/action]`

**Rules**:
- Maximum 155 characters total
- Keyword must appear in the first 60 characters
- Include a number, company name, or year wherever possible
- End with what the reader gains from clicking

**Template**:
`[Keyword]: [Company/Figure] [did/announced/reported] [specific result]. What it means for [audience] in [year].`

**Examples**:
- `AI in finance: JPMorgan cut fraud losses 28% using real-time ML models. What CFOs need to know before their next infrastructure review.` (138 chars)
- `Agentic AI finance: Goldman's autonomous compliance pilot raises SEC questions. The regulatory risk every fintech leader must assess in 2026.` (140 chars)
- `Machine learning financial services: BlackRock's new credit model outperforms legacy scoring by 19%. How institutional investors should respond.` (143 chars)
