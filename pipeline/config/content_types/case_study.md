# Case Study — Content Type Specification

## Identity
- **Funnel position:** Middle to Bottom of Funnel (evidence-based evaluation)
- **Schema type:** Article
- **Word count:** 1,200-2,000 words
- **Reading time:** 6-10 minutes
- **Publishing frequency:** 4x/week (Tuesday+Sunday afternoons, Thursday+Saturday evenings)
- **Purpose:** Proof that AI works (or doesn't) in specific real-world deployments

## Target Personas
- **Primary:** The Operator (VP Operations, Supply Chain Director)
- **Secondary:** The Finance Lead (CFO evaluating investment)
- **Tertiary:** The Tech Lead (CTO assessing technical feasibility)

## H1 Format
`How [Company] [Achieved Specific Outcome] with [AI Approach]`

Examples:
- "How Maersk Cut Container Routing Costs 23% with AI Agent Networks"
- "How Unilever Reduced Hiring Bias 40% Using Structured AI Screening"
- "How a $50M Manufacturer Deployed Predictive Maintenance in 90 Days"

Anti-patterns:
- "A Case Study on AI in Logistics" (no outcome)
- "Company X Uses AI" (no measurable result)

## Mandatory Sections (in order)

### 1. Lede
Company name, industry, outcome metric, and timeline in the first 2 sentences.

### 2. The Challenge
The specific problem the company faced. Quantified: how much it cost, how many people were affected, what the failure mode was. NOT a generic "AI adoption challenge" but a concrete business problem.

### 3. The Approach
What technology they selected. Why (over alternatives they considered). Key technical decisions. Named vendors, platforms, and integration points.

### 4. The Implementation
Timeline with milestones. Team composition. Phases. Budget range (if available). What surprised them during deployment.

### 5. The Results
Before/after metrics (MANDATORY). Named KPIs with specific numbers. Time-to-value. Unexpected benefits or side effects.

### 6. What They'd Do Differently
Lessons learned. Mistakes made. What they underestimated. This section MUST exist, no case study is complete without honest reflection.

### 7. Key Takeaways for [Primary Persona]
3-5 bullet points targeted at the persona who would replicate this.

## Visual Requirements
- **Before/After card:** MANDATORY (the core data point)
- **Timeline:** MANDATORY (implementation phases)
- **Stat-boxes:** Minimum 2 (outcome metrics)
- **Cover image:** Generated SVG, case_study template (hero metric displayed)

## Source Rules
- If based on secondary sources (press releases, earnings calls), explicitly state: "Based on [Company]'s Q2 2026 earnings call and public press releases."
- If based on primary interviews, state: "Based on [Person]'s presentation at [Event]."
- NEVER present second-hand information as if it were a primary interview.
