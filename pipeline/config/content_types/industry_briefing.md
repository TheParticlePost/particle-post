# Industry Briefing — Content Type Specification

## ⚠️ WORD COUNT IS A HARD CEILING

This is industry_briefing. **800-1,200 words. NOT a target — a ceiling.**

If your draft exceeds 1,200 words, you are writing the wrong format. This is a weekly sector roundup, not a deep analysis of a single topic. Three to four developments, one paragraph each, with a brief "what to watch" closer. That's the entire format.

If a single development needs more space than a paragraph, it is not an industry_briefing item — it is its own news_analysis or deep_dive. Pull it out.

## Identity
- **Funnel position:** Top of Funnel (awareness, industry-specific traffic)
- **Schema type:** NewsArticle
- **Word count:** 800-1,200 words (HARD CEILING — see warning above)
- **Reading time:** 4-6 minutes
- **Publishing frequency:** 1x/week (Wednesday evening)
- **Purpose:** Sector-specific AI intelligence roundup for industry operators

## Target Personas
- **Primary:** The Operator (industry-specific: Supply Chain Director, Plant Manager, Hospital Administrator)
- **Secondary:** The People Lead (CHRO in the target industry)

## Industry Rotation
6-week cycle:
1. Logistics & Supply Chain
2. Manufacturing
3. Healthcare
4. Professional Services (consulting, legal, accounting)
5. Retail & E-Commerce
6. Energy & Utilities

Current week's industry = `(week_number % 6) + 1` mapped to the list above.

## H1 Format
`AI in [Industry]: [Number] Developments [Persona] Can't Ignore This Week`

Examples:
- "AI in Logistics: 4 Developments Supply Chain Directors Can't Ignore This Week"
- "AI in Healthcare: 3 Developments Hospital Administrators Should Watch"

## Mandatory Sections (in order)

### 1. Lede
Industry context + count of developments. "This week in AI x [Industry], three developments are reshaping how [persona] approaches [function]."

### 2-4. Development N (one section each, 3-4 developments)
Each development:
- H2: Company name + what happened
- 1-2 paragraphs: factual summary
- **So What:** 1 paragraph, direct advice for the target persona. "If you run a warehouse, this means..."

### 5. The Pattern
What connects these developments. Is there a trend? An acceleration? A warning sign?

### 6. What To Watch Next Week
2-3 upcoming events, announcements, or regulatory deadlines relevant to this industry.

## Visual Requirements
- **Stat-box:** 1 per development (the key number)
- **Summary table:** Recommended (all developments at a glance)
- **Cover image:** Generated SVG, industry_briefing template

## Research Rules
- Must use industry-specific search queries, NOT generic "AI business news"
- Minimum 3 named companies from the target industry
- At least 1 development must come from outside North America (global perspective)
