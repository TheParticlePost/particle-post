# Technology Profile — Content Type Specification

## Identity
- **Funnel position:** Middle of Funnel (evaluation, shortlisting)
- **Schema type:** Article
- **Word count:** 1,500-2,500 words
- **Reading time:** 8-12 minutes
- **Publishing frequency:** 3x/week (Tuesday evening, Thursday+Saturday afternoons)
- **Purpose:** Objective technology assessments for decision-makers evaluating whether a technology category fits their organization

## Target Personas
- **Primary:** The Tech Lead (CTO, VP Engineering assessing architecture)
- **Secondary:** The Operator (VP Ops evaluating operational fit)
- **Tertiary:** The Strategist (CEO making investment decisions)

## H1 Format
`[Technology Category]: What [Persona] Needs to Know in [Year]`

Examples:
- "AI Agent Orchestration Platforms: What CTOs Need to Know in 2026"
- "Computer Vision for Quality Control: What Manufacturing Directors Should Evaluate"
- "AI-Powered ERP Systems: What Operations Leaders Need to Know Before Buying"

## Mandatory Sections (in order)

### 1. What It Is
Plain language definition. No jargon without immediate definition. A supply chain director who's never heard of this technology should understand it after this paragraph.

### 2. How It Works
Architecture-level explanation. NOT code-level. Diagrams encouraged. Think: "data flows in here, processing happens here, output goes here."

### 3. Who Uses It
3+ named companies with specific use cases. Industry distribution. Company size ranges.

### 4. Vendor Landscape
Minimum 2 vendors compared. For each: pricing model, key differentiator, target customer size, maturity level (experimental/production-ready/enterprise-grade).

### 5. Where It Fits
Use cases ranked by maturity. What's production-ready vs. what's still experimental. Honest about what works and what doesn't.

### 6. Risks and Limitations
Specific, honest. Integration challenges, data requirements, skills needed, vendor lock-in risks. This section must NOT read like marketing.

### 7. Our Assessment
When to adopt now, when to wait, when to skip entirely. Specific conditions for each recommendation.

## Visual Requirements
- **Comparison table:** MANDATORY (vendor/platform comparison)
- **Stat-boxes:** Minimum 2 (market size, adoption rate, or ROI data)
- **Maturity spectrum or quadrant:** Recommended
- **Cover image:** Generated SVG, technology_profile template

## Content Rules
- MUST compare at least 2 vendors/approaches, single-vendor profiles read as sponsored content
- Explicit trade-offs required for every recommendation
- Pricing ranges MUST be included (or explicitly noted as "not publicly available")
