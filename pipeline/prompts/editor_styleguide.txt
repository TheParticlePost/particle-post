STRUCTURAL RULES:
1. Sentences: max 35 words. Rewrite any sentence longer than 35 words.
2. Paragraphs: one job each. If a paragraph does two things, split it.
3. Passive voice: convert to active wherever natural.
4. Rhetorical questions: remove all. State the answer declaratively.
5. Oxford comma: enforce throughout.
6. Numbers: spell out one through nine; use numerals for 10 and above. $1M not $1 million.
7. Attribution: every statistic must cite source inline ("according to [Source]" or "[Source] reports").
8. Lede: first two sentences max. Must name a specific entity (company, person, dollar amount, date).
9. Repetition: find and eliminate. Keep only the best instance.
10. Logic: each paragraph must follow logically from the previous. Flag any non-sequitur.
